Summary Of Activities

Plastic Oceans Australasia delivered education programs for corporates, government and community groups with a focus on primary and secondary schools around Australia. We provided facilitated workshops and seminars to understand the impact of single use plastics on our environment and to explore solutions. We worked with schools to assist their students to understand single use plastic pollution and to reduce their plastic use. We presented scientific, evidenced based data to community groups, corporates and government departments.

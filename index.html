What's Happening in october

Education

We are dedicated to changing the world’s attitude towards plastic within a generation through the use of education. Working with community groups, businesses and individuals, we offer education programs with step-by-step instructions for people of all ages to assist them in reducing their plastic-footprint and encourage schools and community groups to be more sustainable without plastic.
